Welcome to blankpa.ge

We're happy to have you here!

This is your blankpage.

Jot notes down. Quickly catch a phone number. Map your thoughts. Paste screenshots for future reference.

If you have comments or feedback, we want to hear from you. Please contact: support_at_blankpa.ge

Blankpa.ge's design goals:
  • Clutter-free: clutter-free UI that gets out of your way
  • Lightweight: the total download must be 10kB
  • Data respect: Your data is your data. All data is stored locally on your machine. If you want to save your notes, hit save to download it to your local drive.
